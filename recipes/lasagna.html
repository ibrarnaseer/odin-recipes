Lasagna

Description

This lasagna with ground beef and whole wheat noodles is an easy, yet hearty family-pleasing dish.

Ingredients:

  • 9 whole wheat lasagna noodles
  • 400g ground beef
  • 2tbsp oil for frying
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 clovers finely chopped garlic
  • 1 tin can chopped tomatoes
  • 2tbsp tomato paste
  • 1dl water
  • 0.5tsp salt
  • 0.5tsp pepper
  • 300g grated cheese

Steps:

  1. Brown beef in frying pan with oil. Add onion, garlic, tomatoes, tomato paste and water. Let it sit for about 10 minutes until the sauce starts to thicken. Add salt and pepper.
  2. Add lasagna noodles and meat sauce in a casserole. Start and end with meat sauce. Add grated cheese on top at the end.
  3. Put casserole in oven at 225 degrees celsius, and cook for 30-40 minutes. Check with a sharp knife if noodles are soft. Let lasagna rest for a few minutes before serving.
