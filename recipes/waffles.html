Waffles

Description:

Mom's amazingly delicious homemade waffles. This recipe will create 6 waffles.

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 110g butter
  • 100g white sugar
  • 1tbsp water
  • 200g wheat flour
  • 1.5 (small) glass whole milk

Steps:

  1. Put eggs and sugar in a bowl and mix.
  2. Melt butter and add into bowl with water. Mix slowly.
  3. Add milk. Mix slowly.
  4. Add flour and mix vigorously.
